# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Solo user — one person tracking their own spending, subscriptions, and budgets, often across multiple currencies. Opens the app deliberately (not passively), usually to log something, check a renewal, or see where the month is going. Context is desktop-first via Electron, occasional browser use. Emotionally, money tracking can feel like a chore; Sack should make it feel light and even satisfying.

## Product Purpose

Sack is a personal finance tracker for subscriptions, expenses, and budgets. It centralises recurring costs, flags what's coming up, and gives a monthly spending picture — without requiring accounting knowledge or financial anxiety. Success looks like: the user opens it, sees exactly what they need in under 10 seconds, and feels in control.

## Brand Personality

Smart, playful, personal.

A tool that feels like it gets you. Quietly witty, never corporate. It knows money is serious but doesn't treat every interaction like a board meeting. The name "Sack" (💰) sets the tone: direct, a little cheeky, memorable.

## Anti-references

- **Spreadsheet clones**: dense tables, utility-only, zero warmth or personality.
- **Crypto/fintech hype**: dark neon, rocket emojis, aggressive CTAs, growth-dashboard energy.

## Design Principles

1. **In control, not overwhelmed.** The most important number is always visible. Secondary data earns its place.
2. **Personality without noise.** Playfulness lives in type, colour, and small details — not decorative chrome or animated clutter.
3. **Money feels lighter here.** Tone is calm and confident. Error states don't panic; empty states encourage.
4. **Honest hierarchy.** Bigger = more important. Never use size or colour to make secondary things look impressive.
5. **Built for one.** Every interaction is personal, not designed for a generic user persona.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AAA where achievable. Priorities: strong contrast (7:1 for body text where possible), full keyboard navigation, reduced-motion support on all animations, colour-blind-safe palette (never red/green alone for status).
